Giving it a go

This website is designed to showcase my learning in HTML, CSS and Webdesign.

Unit 1

So far I have been enjoying Unit 1 of this General Assembly course. I don't know what it is about web design and coding that has been so enjoyable. Perhaps it's the fomulaic nature of coding that has me really excited and engaged. I have to say that the lectures have been very clear and easy to follow.

I'm not yet sure what I'd like my final product to be. Here are some ideas and options:

    Personal blog. I don't have one yet, and am not sure what I would write about. I'm sure I don't need to create a new website in order to start a blog as there are already existing sites, such as weebly and wordpress. However, it would be fun to develop and design a blog my way.
    Resource for current students. I am currently teaching English Language to International High School students, preparing them for the challenges and content that they'll face when they get to High School. Perhaps I can design a website with supplementary material for my current students to support them on their language learning journey.
    Language classes. The final idea I have at this point is to develop a learning resource for my local community. Perhaps I can share my knowledge and skills as an English language and High School teacher digitally with the broader community, without having to allocate a lot of face-to-face time. I have been interested in working with the local and broader community, helping more students access the learning resources they need.
