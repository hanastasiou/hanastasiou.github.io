Climate Change

Everyone is talking about Climate Change these days, and there's good reason for it. Scientists predict that the world as we know it will change dramatically as a result of it.

Key questions to answer.
  1. What is Climate Change? Is it a new thing or has it happened before?
  2. What are the causes of climate change?
  3. What are Greenhouse Gases?
  4. What are the effects of climate change?
  5. What is currently being done around the world to combat climate change?
  6. What do you think should be done about Climate Change? Prepare a 2-4 minute speech to the class reporting your findings and opinion.
