What you'll do...

  • Choose 1 of the projects listed in the Projects section or develop your own project.
  • It should take you about 2 weeks to complete one project.
  • Complete at least 3 projects during your course.

There are 4 main areas you'll be assessed in:

  • Listening
  • Reading
  • Writing
  • Speaking
  • Listen & view audio and videos about a given topic. Answer comprehension questions by listening for main ideas and key details.
  • Read a range of texts on a given topic and find specific details to answer comprehension questions. Analyse main ideas and messages.
  • Write a range of texts about your given topic. Write reports, stories, letters, and blogs using appropriate language and style.
  • Give a talk or presentation about your given topic. Rehearse and develop your pronunciation skills, tone, voice and body langauge.
